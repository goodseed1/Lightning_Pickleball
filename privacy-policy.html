Introduction

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Lightning Pickleball ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Lightning Pickleball (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Create or update your profile
  • Participate in matches, tournaments, or events
  • Communicate with other users
  • Contact us with inquiries

This information may include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, skill level (LPR rating), playing preferences, bio
  • Authentication Data: Information from third-party sign-in services (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interactions, timestamps
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to help you find nearby players and courts

Camera and Photo Library Access

Our App requests access to your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Allow you to take and upload profile pictures
  • Share match photos and moments with your community
  • Capture and share tournament highlights

We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit permission, and we do not use this data for any purpose other than the features you explicitly use.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate match-making with other players
  • Display your profile to other users in the community
  • Send push notifications about matches, tournaments, and community updates
  • Improve our App and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account or our services
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) to store and process your data. Your information is protected using industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Access controls and monitoring

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information (name, profile picture, skill level) is visible to other App users to facilitate community building and match-making
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party services that help us operate the App (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud storage, push notifications
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Google Maps: Location services for finding courts and players

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Update: Correct or update your personal information through the App
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications or location services through your device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@lightningpickleball.com
